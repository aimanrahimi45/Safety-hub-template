## LEGAL REQUIREMENTS & COMPLIANCE

This handbook is specifically developed to ensure compliance with the **OSH Act 1994 (Occupational Safety and Health Act, Amendment 2022)**. 

**Important legal implications:**
- All procedures in this handbook are legally mandated
- Non-compliance may result in penalties up to **RM500,000**
- Potential imprisonment for serious violations
- This handbook serves as your practical guide to meeting legal obligations

As contractors, you are legally required to:
- Ensure safety of your employees while at work
- Provide and maintain a safe work environment
- Identify, assess, and control hazards
- Develop emergency procedures
- Provide adequate training and supervision
- Consult with employees on safety matters

---

## KEY SAFETY PROCEDURES

### 1. Risk Assessment & Permit Systems
- **Complete Job Safety & Environmental Analysis (JSEA)** before starting work
- **Obtain permits** for high-risk activities:
  - Hot work (welding, cutting, grinding)
  - Working at heights above 2 meters
  - Confined space entry
  - Electrical work on live systems
  - Excavation work
  - Work near hazardous substances

**NO WORK SHALL COMMENCE WITHOUT PROPER PERMITS AND RISK ASSESSMENT**

### 2. General Safety Rules
- Report all defects, hazards, and incidents promptly
- Keep walkways clear at all times
- Wear required personal protective equipment (PPE)
- Know locations of fire extinguishers
- Check electrical tools before use
- Don't work in the dark – arrange sufficient lighting
- Watch where you're walking – don't run

### 3. Prohibited Behaviors
For everyone's safety and security, the following are strictly prohibited:
- Practical jokes and silly antics
- Fighting or threatening behavior
- Intoxication from drugs or alcohol
- Abuse or damage to company property
- Interfering with safety controls or guards
- Failing to follow safety procedures

### 4. Substance Policies
- **Strictly no illegal drugs** at work
- **No alcohol or drug influence** when reporting for work
- **Strictly non-smoking** within company premises
- Follow proper chemical handling procedures
- Dispose of waste properly (general and scheduled waste)

---

## SPECIALIZED SAFETY REQUIREMENTS

### Working at Heights & Confined Spaces
- Use proper fall protection equipment above 2 meters
- Inspect all safety equipment before use
- Secure all tools to prevent dropping
- For confined spaces: obtain permits, test atmosphere, maintain ventilation
- Station attendant outside at all times
- Emergency rescue plan must be in place

### Equipment & Tool Safety
- Use correct tools for the job
- Inspect all tools before use
- Power tools must have current electrical safety tags
- Follow Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedures
- Only authorized personnel may perform LOTO

### Emergency Procedures
- Know site warning systems and escape routes
- Know assembly areas in emergencies
- Understand fire protection procedures
- Know who the first aiders are and where supplies are located
- Report all workplace injuries immediately

## CONTRACTOR MANAGEMENT & COMPLIANCE

### Mandatory Requirements
- Valid business registration and insurance coverage
- Competent person certification for specialized work
- Regular safety meetings with all workers
- Maintain training records for all personnel
- Submit weekly safety reports

### Sub-contractor Management
- Principal contractor is responsible for all sub-contractors
- All sub-contractors must complete this handbook acknowledgment
- Coordinate safety activities between multiple contractors
- Ensure sub-contractors have required permits and training

### Penalties & Enforcement
⚠️ **NON-COMPLIANCE PENALTIES:**
- Safety violation: **RM 100 per incident**
- Repeated violations: **Contract suspension**
- Serious safety breach: **Contract termination**
- Legal violations: **Prosecution under OSH Act 1994**
