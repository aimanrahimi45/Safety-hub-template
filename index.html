Getting Started Is Simple

Install our extension inside your Google Sheets, run the setup, and connect your new dashboard console in minutes.

1

Open Google Sheets

Open any of your existing spreadsheets, or create a brand-new blank sheet in your Google Account.

2

Install the AmerisPro Add-on

Go to Extensions → Add-ons → Get Add-ons, search for AmerisPro, and install the extension.

3

Initialize Your Workspace

Once installed, click on Extensions → AmerisPro → Initialize Workspace to automatically generate all database tabs.

4

Connect to Your Dashboard

Copy the Spreadsheet ID from the URL bar and paste it in the AmerisPro console web portal to log in.
