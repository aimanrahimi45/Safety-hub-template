Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AmerisPro ("we," "our," or "us") handles data when you use the AmerisPro Google Sheets Add-on and its associated web portals (collectively, the "Service").

1. Data Ownership and Storage

AmerisPro is built on a serverless, multi-tenant architecture designed to prioritize your privacy:

  • Your Sheets, Your Data: All safety records, inventory quantities, PPE check logs, contractor signatures, and system configurations are stored directly within your own Google Drive and your own Google Sheets.
  • No Central Database Hosting: We do not host your safety data on external central servers. We cannot access, view, or modify your safety logs or files unless explicitly authorized within your own Google account context.

2. Information We Access

To function properly, the Service requests access to the following Google Workspace permissions (OAuth Scopes):

  • Google Sheets: Used to read and write safety checklist data, PPE logs, and contractor registrations to the database spreadsheets you own.
  • Google Drive: Used to safely upload contractor/visitor signatures and photos to a folder inside your own Google Drive.
  • External Requests: Used to communicate with our secure licensing server to validate your premium purchase license key.

3. License Validation

When you register a license key within your system settings, the add-on sends the license key and your active Spreadsheet ID to our secure validation server to verify the validity of your subscription. This validation does not transmit any of your safety records, employee details, or personal files.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with third parties. Your data remains entirely within your Google account and under your control.

AmerisPro's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Protection Mechanisms

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures to protect your sensitive data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communications between the AmerisPro add-on, Google Sheets, and our license validation server are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS (HTTPS) protocols.
  • Google Secure Infrastructure: The add-on runs entirely within Google’s own application hosting environment (Google Apps Script), benefiting from Google's multi-layered security controls.
  • Minimal Permissions: We request only the narrowest possible OAuth scopes (such as drive.file) needed to perform system actions. Your credentials and access tokens are handled securely by Google and are never visible to or stored by us.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your safety logs and files remain inside your own Google Drive and Sheets, you have complete control over how long your data is stored and when it is deleted:

  • No Centralized Retention: We do not store, cache, or replicate your Google Workspace data (spreadsheets, safety logs, uploaded signatures) on our own databases or servers. Therefore, we do not retain your data.
  • User-Controlled Deletion: You can delete your data (including the databases and signature files) at any time by manually deleting them from your Google Sheets or Google Drive.
  • Access Revocation: You can revoke AmerisPro's access to your Google Account at any time via your Google Account Permissions Settings. Once access is revoked, AmerisPro loses all ability to interact with your files.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at your support email address.
